    SATURDAY OCTOBER 1, 2016

    Rain date: Saturday October 22, 2016

    Ride Distance: 25/50 Miles

    Start/Finish Location:

    Timberville, VA
    17768 Honeyville Rd

    Join us for a pleasantly challenging group ride (25 and 50 mile options) on the rolling country roads around the picturesque Broadway/Timberville area, at the base of the Appalachian mountains. The rides begin promptly at 2:00PM (50 mile) and 4:00PM (25 mile) from Showalter's Orchard, and return to the orchard for a farm table supper set on a hill amidst the apple trees. overlooking the farm on the rolling hills below.

    The meal will feature fresh ingredients straight off the farm, sourced from Showalter's as well as other farms and producers in the Broadway/Timberville area. Old Hill Hard Cider is made on the premises and will be served with the meal. Please let us know ahead of time if you have any food allergies so we can make any necessary substitutions.

    IMPORTANT DATES, DEADLINES & INFO

    Day-of registration is NOT available due to the nature of the event
