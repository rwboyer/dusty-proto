    The Ride

    There are two distance options for this event, 25 miles and 50 miles. Both are pleasantly challenging, self-guided group rides on the rolling country roads around the Broadway/Timberville area, at the base of the Allegheny Mountains. Route markers will be provided for guidance along the way, and the route consists of approximately 95-100% paved roads, with a short section of gravel possible. The 50 mile ride begins promptly at 1:30pm, and the 25 miler at 3:30pm, both from Showalter's Orchard.

    AFTER THE RIDE

    Upon returning to the orchard the event will shift gears into a relaxing meal and evening on the farm. The meal will be a farm table supper set on a hill amidst the apple trees, overlooking the rolling hills of the orchard below. The food will be prepared by Taste. A Food Company., will include fresh ingredients straight off the farm (sourced from Showalter's and other farms in the Broadway/Timberville area) and will be served family-style. Old Hill Hard Cider is made on the premises and will be available for purchase with the meal. Please let us know ahead of time if you have any food allergies so we can make any necessary substitutions.
