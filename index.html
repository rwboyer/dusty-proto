Off the Eaten Path is a series of road cycling events in the Shenandoah Valley of Virginia that pairs our passion for riding bikes with our love of local food and drink. When ridden consecutively, each ride increases incrementally in mileage, culminating with a century (100-ish) mile) ride at the end of the season featuring multiple stops to rest, eat, drink and enjoy life with your riding mates.

Join us for one, several or all of our events this year, each starting and finishing at one of the valleyâ€™s finest local farms or vineyards. All events include a pleasantly challenging group ride of varying distance followed by an authentic farm-to-fork supper prepared by local caterer Taste. A Food Company. Each meal is made from fresh, local ingredients and paired with our favorite local beverages.
