Off the Eaten Path is a series of road cycling events in the Shenandoah Valley of Virginia that pair our passion for riding bikes with our love of local food and drink. Each event begins and ends at one of the valley's finest local farms and includes a pleasantly challenging group ride followed by an authentic farm table supper prepared by local caterer Taste. A Food Company. Each meal is made from fresh ingredients sourced from local producers and is paired with some of our favorite local beverages.
